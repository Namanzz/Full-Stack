The Best Movies According to Naman

My top 3 movies of all-time.

3 Idiots

It is a heartwarming Bollywood film that celebrates friendship, innovation, and the pursuit of passion over societal expectations.

Iron-Man

"Iron Man" is a 2008 superhero film that launched the Marvel Cinematic Universe, featuring Tony Stark, a genius billionaire who becomes a hero in a high-tech armored suit.

365 Days

This Polish romantic drama film is a beautiful and intense portrayal of love, passion and a lot of sex.
